Serving Many Reputable Customers

MATRICO have worked side by side with reputable customers within the automation industry who profit from our experience with casting methods, processing, production, surface treatments and quality control. Our supply chain lets us choose from different types of castings to satisfy the demand.
We offers a comprehensive range of automation products and services designed to optimize manufacturing processes and drive operational excellence in the machine industry.

Design

MATRICO design services are aimed at enabling clients in the automated machine industry to develop innovative, efficient, and reliable automation solutions that meet their specific needs and requirements.
In Matrico, we encourage collaborative ideation sessions to conceptualize automation solutions tailored to specific manufacturing needs. Creation of preliminary sketches, 3D models, and prototypes to visualize design concepts and explore feasibility. Our commitment to continuous refinement and optimization of machine designs through iterative testing, analysis, and feedback-driven improvements.

Manufacture

MATRICO manufacturing services are tailored to meet the diverse needs of clients in the automated machine industry, enabling them to efficiently produce high-quality components and assemblies for automation systems.
Expertise in welding various materials, including steel, aluminum, stainless steel, and exotic alloys, we encourage comprehensive quality assurance processes to meet specific application requirements, including in-process inspections, dimensional checks, and functional testing, to ensure product conformance and reliability. Provision of post-sales support services, including installation assistance, operator training, and technical support, to ensure smooth deployment and operation of automated machines. In addition, scheduled maintenance programs and spare parts management services to maximize uptime, prolong equipment lifespan, and minimize downtime for our clients.

Services

MATRICO maintenance and service solutions are designed to help clients in the automated machine industry maximize the uptime, reliability, and longevity of their equipment, ultimately improving productivity and profitability.

Rapid response emergency repair services to address unexpected equipment failures, minimizing downtime and production losses show our commitment to grow together with our clients. Retrofitting of outdated components, control systems, and software to improve reliability, productivity, and compliance with industry standards.
